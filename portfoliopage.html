A tribute to the city that raised me


"Milan has always been my home, even though I was not born here, I was raised and shaped by this city. Here, I discovered that every person and place in Milan has a story to tell, from the ancient buildings to the new graffiti on the collone, and from the exchange students trying their first aperitivo to the locals reminiscing about the "good old days." This city holds a special place in my heart.
